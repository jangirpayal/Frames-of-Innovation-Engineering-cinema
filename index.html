Essential Nutrition Information

Macronutrients

Proteins

Build and repair tissues. Aim for 0.8g per kg of body weight daily.

  • Lean meats and fish
  • Eggs and dairy
  • Legumes and nuts

Carbohydrates

Primary energy source. Choose complex carbs for sustained energy.

  • Whole grains
  • Fruits and vegetables
  • Legumes

Healthy Fats

Essential for hormone production and nutrient absorption.

  • Avocados and olive oil
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Fatty fish

Vitamins & Minerals

Vitamin C

Immune support, collagen production

Citrus fruits, berries, bell peppers

Vitamin D

Bone health, immune function

Fatty fish, fortified milk, sunlight

Iron

Oxygen transport, energy production

Red meat, spinach, lentils

Calcium

Bone and teeth health

Dairy, leafy greens, almonds

Portion Control

Protein (Palm Size)

3-4 oz serving, about the size of your palm

Vegetables (Fist Size)

1-2 cups, about the size of your fist

Carbs (Cupped Hand)

1/2 cup, about the size of your cupped hand

Fats (Thumb Size)

1 tbsp, about the size of your thumb
